FAQ

When do I need to have a subscription?

Only when you're actively selling your game/application. Even if you're making a game that you plan to sell, you don't have to have to a subscription until it's being sold.

However, there are perks to having an active subscription whether you sell your game or not and we greatly appreciate the support.

Do I need a subscription per game?

Nope. You only need one active subscription per person or per organization. Make and sell as many games as you want.

Can I make changes to the OPengine?

Absolutely. Change it to your hearts content. Make a pull request and we'll get your changes merged into the main branch. Or keep the changes to yourself.

The only stipulation is you can't resell/redistribute the modified OPengine outside of your organization.

Will the subscription price increase?

As the OPengine improves over time there will likely be a price increase, however your monthly rate will be fixed at the time you begin your subscription. We still retain the right to change the pricing cost and/or pricing model at a future date and time.
